Compare total cost, not just the headline

Some providers include consultations, delivery, and aftercare. Others charge extra. Always check what's bundled before ordering.

📈

Starter vs. maintenance doses

You start low (e.g. 2.5mg Mounjaro) and titrate up over months. Pricing differences are largest at higher doses like 10–15mg.

🏥

In-store vs. online

Boots and Asda offer in-store collection. Online-only providers often undercut high street prices but won't offer face-to-face support.

🔒

Check GPhC registration

Only use pharmacies registered with the General Pharmaceutical Council. Verify any UK pharmacy at pharmacyregulation.org.

How to ask for a deal
Most providers don't publicly advertise reactivation offers, but will offer them when asked. Try:

1. Email or live chat your previous provider
2. Say you're considering returning but found a cheaper alternative
3. Ask if they have any return or loyalty discount
4. Check your email inbox — lapsed patient offers are often sent automatically after 30–60 days of inactivity
